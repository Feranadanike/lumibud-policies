Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

1. Scope: Mobile App and Web

This policy covers both the Lumibud mobile app and any Lumibud web experience. On mobile we use device storage (not browser cookies); on web we use minimal cookies.

2. Mobile App: Device Storage

The Lumibud mobile app does not use cookies. It may use secure storage and local storage for authentication/session state and preferences (e.g. theme, settings). We do not use advertising or cross-app tracking.

3. Web: Cookies

If you use Lumibud on the web, we use minimal, essential cookies for core functionality (such as authentication and session management). We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

What we do not use: ads cookies, third-party trackers, cross-site tracking, behavioral profiling cookies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

Lumibud uses Supabase for backend and authentication. On web, Supabase may set cookies for session management.
