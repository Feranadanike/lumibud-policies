Account Deletion

Last updated: April 21, 2026

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Lumibud app
  2. Go to Profile
  3. Scroll to Danger Zone
  4. Tap Delete My Account and follow the prompts
Important: Account deletion is intended to be permanent and cannot be undone.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, we delete your account record and associated data stored in Lumibud where applicable (for example: tasks, goals, habits, journal entries, bills, trials, deadlines, and other in-app records linked to your account).

When deletion happens

We aim to permanently remove your account and associated data within 30 days of your deletion request.

Need help?

If you can’t access the app or need assistance deleting your account, contact us at privacylumibud@gmail.com.
