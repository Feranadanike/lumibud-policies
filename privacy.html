Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the Lumibud mobile application and any related web or extension services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and explaining how we collect, use, and disclose information.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Account information (email, name)
  • Tasks, goals, routines, habits, and deadlines
  • Journal entries, notes, and saved links
  • Mood tracking and wellness data
  • Financial records (expenses, bills, trials)
  • Study Corner: files you upload, summaries, quizzes, and study notes
  • Book Club: membership, display name, posts, progress, and bookmarks you choose to share
  • Buddies: group membership and share settings
  • Profile preferences (theme, currency, settings)

Automatically collected information:

  • Device type, operating system, and app version (for compatibility and support)
  • Error logs for debugging and improving the app
  • Push notification token(s) (e.g. Expo push tokens) if you enable notifications, used only to deliver notifications to your device
No ads / tracking: We do not use third-party advertising or analytics that track you across apps or sites.

AI-Powered Features (Study Corner)

Study Corner may use third-party AI providers (e.g. OpenAI) to generate summaries, quizzes, and study help. Text or content you submit may be sent to the AI provider to generate responses. We do not use your content to train AI models; processing is for your session only. See the AI provider’s privacy policy for their practices.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Lumibud service
  • To personalize your experience
  • To analyze and improve the app
  • To communicate important updates
  • To deliver notifications you enable (reminders and updates)
  • To ensure security and prevent fraud
  • To provide customer support

4. Data Storage and Security

We use Supabase as our backend provider. We use industry-standard security measures such as encryption in transit (SSL/TLS) and encryption at rest.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect rights, safety, and security
  • With service providers who help us operate Lumibud (e.g. Supabase for hosting and authentication; Expo for push notification delivery; AI providers for Study Corner features when you use them)

6. Your Rights

You may have rights such as access, rectification, erasure, portability, restriction, objection, and withdrawal of consent.

7. Push Notifications

If you enable notifications, Lumibud may send reminders and updates (for example: bills due/overdue, trials to cancel, tasks and deadlines, goal reminders, birthdays, and Buddy activity where applicable). You can turn notifications off in your device settings.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can delete your account in the app; this will permanently remove your account and associated data within 30 days.

9. Cookies, Storage, and Tracking

Mobile: device storage for authentication/session state and preferences. No advertising or cross-app tracking.
Web (if applicable): minimal cookies for authentication/session management and preferences.

10. International Data Transfers

Lumibud may process and store information using service providers that operate in different countries. When transferring information internationally, we take steps designed to protect your information consistent with applicable law.

11. U.S. State Privacy Rights (e.g., California)

If you live in certain U.S. states (including California), you may have additional rights regarding your personal information. Lumibud does not sell personal information and does not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

12. Children’s Privacy

Lumibud is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and will update the “Last updated” date.
